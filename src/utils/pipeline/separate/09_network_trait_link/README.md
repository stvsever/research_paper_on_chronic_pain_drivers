# 09 - Baseline profiles and the momentary experience of pain (RQ2)

`01_between_person.py` relates each participant's mean momentary attention to pain and mean goal
engagement to the threat, biomedical, and personality profiles, with zero-order and partial
(controlling mean pain) correlations and a hierarchical regression. `02_moderation.R` tests, in
one-step multilevel models with random slopes, whether a baseline profile moderates the
within-person coupling strengths, replacing the underpowered two-step correlation of per-person
coefficients with baseline traits.
